## Animation Approach

The collaborative process involves:

- **Character Movement**: Bringing illustrated characters to life with personality-driven motion
- **Timing & Rhythm**: Carefully crafted animation timing for visual impact
- **Collaborative Process**: Working with other artists to combine strengths and styles
- **Technical Execution**: Converting static art into animated sequences

<Note>
  Animation projects combine digital illustration skills with motion design principles to create engaging visual experiences.
</Note>

## Creative Process

Animation work builds on the foundation of strong character design and illustration, adding the dimension of time and movement to tell more dynamic stories.
