## What is osu! catch?

osu! catch (formerly known as Catch the Beat) is one of the four game modes in osu!, a free-to-play rhythm game. In osu! catch, players control a character at the bottom of the screen who catches falling fruits to the beat of the music.

<Info>
osu! catch requires exceptional reaction time, pattern recognition, and finger control to catch increasingly complex patterns of fruits falling at high speeds.
</Info>

## Gameplay Mechanics

In osu! catch, players must:

- **Catch fruits** - Move left and right to catch normal fruits
- **Catch droplets** - Smaller drops that appear between fruits
- **Collect bananas** - Bonus items during spinner sections
- **Catch hyperfruit** - Special fruits that trigger a dash to reach distant patterns

The objective is to maintain accuracy and combo throughout the song while navigating increasingly difficult patterns.
