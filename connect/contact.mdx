  Response times may vary depending on Max's schedule and the nature of your inquiry. Please be patient and provide clear context in your message.

## Tips for Reaching Out

- **Be specific**: Clearly state the purpose of your message and what you're looking for
- **Provide context**: Include relevant details about your project, question, or proposal
- **Be respectful**: Max receives many messages, so patience is appreciated
- **Choose the right platform**: Use Discord for quick questions, Twitter for professional inquiries

## For Professional Opportunities

If you're reaching out regarding:
- **Collaboration opportunities**: Provide details about the project and your timeline
- **Technical questions**: Include relevant code snippets or error messages
- **Content suggestions**: Share your ideas and why you think they'd be valuable

<Note>
  Max is open to collaborations and interesting projects. Don't hesitate to reach out with genuine opportunities or questions!
</Note>
